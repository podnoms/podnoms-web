We ask for your email address in order to uniquely identify the podcasts you subscribe to.
We may in the future send you emails when your subscribed podcasts have been updated but if we do you will be able to turn these off here

You may, if you wish, add your full name (assuming we have not been able to get this from a social login provider). This is only used for displaying a greeting in the website when you are logged in. You can change this to anything you want.

In short - we collect the bare minimum, we don't really care who you are or what you are up to as long as you find this site useful.
